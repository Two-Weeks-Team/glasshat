Act II · The Turn

Then Yellow says "9 out of 10"…
and the room goes quiet.

The detector flags it. Phoenix consults the past. Qdrant pulls anchors from 503 prior submissions. Watch the score correct itself, in front of you.

Act III · The Proof

Same submission. Two rubrics.
Two correct scores.

Glasshat synthesizes the evaluator's actual rules into a per-evaluation rubric. The same project scores 87 under Qdrant's weights and 73 under Rapid Agent's weights — and both are right. Fairness is rubric-relative.

Qdrant VSD Rubric
87
Originality · Functionality · UX · Material Vector Use
weights: orig 30 · func 30 · ux 20 · vector 20
Rapid Agent / Arize Rubric
73
Technical Execution · Innovation · Impact · Presentation
weights: tech 40 · innov 30 · impact 20 · pres 10

"Correct rubric-aware variance, not bias." — the dual-rubric reveal at 2:30.
