Trust & Safety

Security & privacy

The controls your CISO will ask for in the first call

Glasshat is built to be deployable in regulated environments. We don't ship product features that block your security review.

SAML 2.0 SSO & OIDC

Okta, Azure AD/Entra ID, Google Workspace, Ping, JumpCloud. JIT provisioning, group-claim role mapping, enforced MFA passthrough.

SCIM 2.0 user lifecycle

Auto-provision & auto-deprovision via your IdP. Group-based access policies. Token-scoped service accounts for CI/CD.

Append-only audit log

Every action — who, what, when, from where. Export to SIEM via streaming connectors (Splunk, Datadog, Sumo Logic). WORM-compatible storage adapter included.

Data residency & isolation

Pick your region: US, EU (Frankfurt), APAC (Tokyo). VPC-peered single-tenant available on Enterprise. BYOK with AWS KMS / GCP KMS / Azure Key Vault.

Encryption everywhere

TLS 1.3 in transit. AES-256-GCM at rest. Per-tenant data-encryption keys derived from envelope KMS root. Quarterly key rotation.

Role-based access control

4 built-in roles: admin, reviewer, auditor (read-only + export), service. Custom roles via policy DSL. Per-rubric and per-project scoping.

Runtime introspection (Phoenix MCP)

Every agent call, retrieval, and score change is OpenInference-instrumented. Reviewers replay any decision — same inputs, same model snapshot, same verdict — for post-hoc compliance review.

Legal & contracts

DPA (GDPR Art. 28) standard. BAA for HIPAA workloads on request. Sub-processor list published in Trust Center. 30-day breach notification SLA.
