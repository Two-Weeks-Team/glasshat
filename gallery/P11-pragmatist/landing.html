Concept lineage: the Six Thinking Hats decomposition, the BMAD 17-item rubric and the 3D evaluation graph derive from fairthon.com (same team) as design/spec. No fairthon source code is reused; every line in this repo is freshly authored 2026-05-13 onward.

Why this page is boring on purpose

Three-year-old SaaS layout. No animations. No Three.js. No CDN fonts. No marketing copy. Stage 1 graders read top-to-bottom; this page lets them tick boxes in 90 seconds. The wow moments live in the demo timeline — and even those degrade to 2D radar if WebGL fails on the judge's laptop.
